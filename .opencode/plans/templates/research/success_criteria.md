### Purpose
Define concrete, measurable completion conditions for the research effort, including decision readiness.

### Required-content checklist
- [ ] Quantitative success thresholds are defined.
- [ ] Qualitative acceptance conditions are stated.
- [ ] Reproducibility evidence requirements are listed.
- [ ] Final recommendation deliverable is specified.

### Drafter prompts
- What outcomes are necessary versus merely desirable?
- What evidence is required for peer review sign-off?
- How will success be communicated to decision-makers?

### Example
Success requires statistically significant improvement over baseline on two primary metrics, reproducible rerun within tolerance on independent hardware, and a written recommendation with rollout/deferral rationale.
