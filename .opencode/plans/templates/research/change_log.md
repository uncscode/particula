### Purpose
Maintain a chronological record of substantive plan updates to support traceability and reproducibility.

### Required-content checklist
- [ ] Entries include date, author, and summary of change.
- [ ] Rationale for each significant change is captured.
- [ ] Impacts on hypotheses, scope, or evaluation are noted.
- [ ] Links to related artifacts/issues are provided where applicable.

### Drafter prompts
- What changed since the previous review checkpoint?
- Why was this change necessary?
- Which downstream artifacts must be updated as a consequence?

### Example
| Date | Change | Author |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2026-06-01 | Narrowed evaluation cohort to remove a data-drift confounder; updated success thresholds and dependency timeline; linked issue `#2808`. | `research-owner` |
