### Purpose
Define the experimental design, controls, and analysis methods so findings are statistically and operationally credible.

### Required-content checklist
- [ ] Independent/dependent variables are defined.
- [ ] Control and comparison conditions are documented.
- [ ] Sampling strategy and run counts are specified.
- [ ] Statistical or analytical methods are justified.

### Drafter prompts
- What is the minimum experiment set needed to evaluate the hypothesis?
- How are random effects controlled or measured?
- What pre-registered analysis choices prevent p-hacking?

### Example
Run 30 seeded trials per condition, compare baseline vs intervention using bootstrap confidence intervals for latency and paired significance testing for quality metrics, and pre-define exclusion criteria for unstable runs.
