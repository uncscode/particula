### Purpose
Track unresolved decisions and knowledge gaps that must be answered to keep the research plan executable.

### Required-content checklist
- [ ] Each question has an owner.
- [ ] Decision deadline or review checkpoint is listed.
- [ ] Blocking vs non-blocking status is labeled.
- [ ] Resolution path (experiment, review, or dependency) is identified.

### Drafter prompts
- Which unanswered question can invalidate the current methodology?
- What evidence will close each question?
- Which team or reviewer must approve the resolution?

### Example
Question: Should calibration error be promoted to a primary metric? Owner: Applied Science Lead. Deadline: before checkpoint C2. Status: blocking. Resolution path: run calibration-focused evaluation on two representative cohorts.
