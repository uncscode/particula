### Purpose
Summarize the research problem, primary hypothesis, and intended scientific or engineering outcomes.

### Required-content checklist
- [ ] Problem statement is explicit and scoped.
- [ ] Primary hypothesis (or guiding research question) is stated.
- [ ] Expected impact and decision value are described.
- [ ] Success signals are stated at a high level.

### Drafter prompts
- What decision will this research unlock if successful?
- Which assumptions are being tested first?
- What would falsify the hypothesis quickly?

### Example
For a model-compression investigation, define the hypothesis that structured pruning can reduce inference latency by 30% while keeping F1-score loss under 1%, and explain that results will guide whether to ship an edge inference profile.
